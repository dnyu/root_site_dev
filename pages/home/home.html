    • Bridging Two Worlds

  • Turing's mission is to produce simultaneously cost-efficient and environmentally-friendly technologies.
  • Economic development has historically been at direct odds with environmental protection.
  • Thus we made Root so that you achieve immediate financial savings while contributing to environmental sustainability.

    • The Power of Networks

  • One Root can already save substantial energy with minimal effort.
  • However, energy savings grow exponentially as the number of Roots in the same network increases.
  • Getting a second root means the first Root is no longer limited to it's own data input, giving it more information to optimize over.
  • As Turing's algorithms gather more information to learn from, they'll progressively become smarter and more capable of producing cutting edge optimization metrics for your energy.
