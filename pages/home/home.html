Enter your home, pre-cooled (or pre-heated) just for your arrival.


Get reminded that you left your TV idle. And tap a button to turn it off.


Choose your max electricity bill.
And let Root's optimization algorithms make the most of it.





Instant.





Plug Root into any power outlet.
Connect any appliance to Root.







Install the Turing App on your smartphone.
Wirelessly control your Root.






Choose from many optimization and automation settings.
Watch your energy bill get uprooted.




Powerful.


Our completely decentralized software stack minimizes Root's power consumption and makes network crashes impossible.






A decentralized server and database means no one (including us) can access any of your data.




Getting another Root means each Root in your network gets more data to optimize over, leading to an exponentially improving smarthome.




















Progress Log


March 2016: We have finished designing and implementing an initial framework for the software and backend. We've constructed a basic scheduling option that allows users to customize a 24-hour schedule for their ROOT to automatically follow.

February 2016: We built a minimum viable prototype that can control the flow of electricity through an outlet via the press of a button. We looking for a summer accelerator program so that we can work full time on Root.

January 2016: We won a 75+ team competition for admission to the WeissLabs Accelerator at Penn Engineering. We now have space and mentorship by industry experts.

December 2015: We got the idea for Root and did all of the research to ensure that it is buildable and cost efficient.
