Saving Energy is the Future

Short-term Long-term:

Saving energy is an idea that has no true drawbacks. It intersects two seemingly opposite ideas of immediate payback and long-term benefits. ROOT allows for immediate financial savings, as well as long term preservation of the earth's environment. This rare opportunity is a chance that should be taken advantage by everyone.

Big Data Potential:

"Big Data is an enormous opportunity for making environmental improvements and harnessing energy-efficiency savings. We seem to observe an 'energy-efficiency gap': People do not seem to adopt efficient technologies that appear financially attractive. One of the commonly cited reasons is that information about how to save energy is hard or time- consuming to collect, or that the efficient devices are hard to use. Firms that employ Big Data can help consumers overcome these challenges." - Wharton Business Economics and Public Policy Professor, Arthur van Benthem

A Network Benefits From Itself:

Clearly, one ROOT is able to help the environment while saving money. However, by having multiple ROOTs connected to the same network, the potential skyrockets. Each ROOT is no longer limited to their own scope, but are also able to interact and communicate with other ROOTS in the same network.
